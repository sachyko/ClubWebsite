Who We Are

Our 3Cs:

Communicate | Culture | Community

We host daily multi-language Exchange and International Meet ups. With participants from more than 100 countries, an incredible way for language learners to give and take away language skills. It's also an opportunity to teach your own culture to foreigners and learn about differnent culture from other countries.

The Learners

Speakers of all levels are welcome

Seats change every 30 munites with maximum of 5 people. Tables are according to your own degree of language fluency, so you can feel free to participate in a very cozy environment.

Languages

8 languages that you can learn

We regularly hold meet-ups in 8 languages other than English and Japanese, and we also have free time every day at 9pm, where you can speak any language.
